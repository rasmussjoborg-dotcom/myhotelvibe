# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Discerning travelers seeking luxury, design-forward, and highly curated hotel experiences. They value aesthetics, insider knowledge, and an elevated "vibe" over generic utility.

## Product Purpose
An editorial-driven hotel discovery platform that curates exceptional stays across Europe. It serves as a tastemaker's notebook, helping users find not just a room, but a specific aesthetic and lifestyle experience.

## Brand Personality
Editorial, authoritative, elegant, minimalist. Think of a high-end travel magazine (Monocle, Condé Nast) crossed with an insider's black book.

## Anti-references
Booking.com, Expedia, standard SaaS dashboards, bloated grey UI containers, generic travel agency sites. It should explicitly avoid the "SaaS metric" or "dashboard card" aesthetic.

## Design Principles
- Let the text breathe: Rely on typography and white space rather than containing prose in colored boxes.
- Structured Elegance: Use sleek, thin borders and clean dividers for structured data (like restaurants or facts) to create a premium "index" feel.
- Expert Curation: The UI must frame the content as expert advice, using editorial markers (drop caps, elegant subheadings, refined spacing).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain high contrast (WCAG AA minimum) specifically avoiding light-gray-on-white text which plagues "elegant" designs. Ensure interactive elements are clearly discernible.
